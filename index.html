About BeatsByBOOM

BeatzByBoom aka UT Boom is a Nigerian reccord producer, sound engineer and computer engineer who hails from Akwa Ibom state. Born Utibeima Ubom, BeatzByBoom is best known as an afrobeat producer/musician who specializes in afro-fusion, afro-soul and core afrobeats genre of music. With over 20+ official records to his name under the moniker “UT Boom”, BeatzByBoom is emerging as one of Africa's fast rising music producer offering unique sounds and versatility to his musical producing prowess. BeatzbyBoom started production at the age of 16 and since then, has matured over the years and has gone on to produce for the likes of Magnito, Jmizi, A.jay oddlad etc . He is expected to be a big name in the music industry scene soonest as He possesses the skills and talent to make that possible. You can connect with BeatzByBoom on social media below;
